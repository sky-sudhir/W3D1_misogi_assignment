Q: What is diabetes?
A: Diabetes is a chronic condition where the body cannot properly regulate blood sugar levels.

Q: What causes high blood pressure?
A: High blood pressure can be caused by stress, poor diet, lack of exercise, or genetics.

Q: What are the symptoms of dengue?
A: Dengue symptoms include high fever, severe headache, joint pain, and skin rash.
